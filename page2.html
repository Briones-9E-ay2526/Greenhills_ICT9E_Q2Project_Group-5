Courses Offered

At O.B. Montessori Center, we offer comprehensive Montessori education programs designed to nurture the whole child at every developmental stage.

Casa (Ages 3-5)

Our foundational Montessori program for preschoolers, focusing on developing independence, concentration, and coordination through practical life exercises.

Primary Grade School (Ages 6-8)

Building on Casa foundations, this program encourages exploration, critical thinking, and collaborative learning through interdisciplinary studies.

Intermediate Grade School (Ages 9-12)

Designed for upper elementary students, this program emphasizes research skills, project-based learning, and community engagement.

Junior High School (Ages 13-16)

Our adolescent program combines rigorous academics with real-world applications, entrepreneurship, and community involvement.

Senior High School (Ages 17-18)

College preparatory program with specialized tracks aligned with students' interests and career goals.
